Vani

🙌 Summary

Books are a uniquely portable magic. - Stephen King

This quote beautifully describes what wonders books can do in our lives. Why not use books as a medium to support, appreciate our friends, colleagues and even strangers.

Introducing the Vani Bot, to which you can make wishes in form of books and they might get fulfilled. Using Vani Bot will help in improving team bonding, creating a sense of fulfilment ultimately making the remote social environment livelier.

🙇‍♀️ Problem Statement

🧳 Use cases

  • To support community members on Mattermost.
  • To show appreciation to friends, colleagues and even strangers by gifting them books of their choice.

🚴‍♀️ Approach

🎮 Workflow

  1. Registering to Vani.
  1. Gifting when both people are using vani.
  1. Gifting when the other is not there On Vani.
